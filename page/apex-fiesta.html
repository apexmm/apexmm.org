Apex Fiesta 2K17

Apex fiesta is the annual function program of APS. This year APS is celebrating 2ndannual function on March 20,2016. The students of APS prepared many cultural programs for this auspicious day such as song, dance, skit etc. The program is managed by students of cultural department under the guidance of Cultural dept. faculty. On this day, The School Managing committee announces the Mega Annual Awards" such as Student Of The Year,Best faculty of the year, Best transport facility of the year award etc.
