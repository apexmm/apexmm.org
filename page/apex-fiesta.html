APEX FIESTA 2017

APEX FIESTA is the annual fest of Apex Public School. Apex fiesta was started in the year 2015. APS organizes different inter school competition such as Quiz, Speech, Debate, drawing etc under APEX FIESTA on the ocassion Of Foundation Day.
The purpose of organizing these events to provide oppurtunity to students to show their etenal talent. It provides staze to students to compete with students of different school which will be helpful in developing personality. This year also following events are going to organize:

Rules and Regulations:

  • For participating in any event schools resgistartion is mandatory. Without registration students of respective school will not be eligible to participate.
  • Individual student or group of two students can participate.
  • Details of two coordinators(teachers) must be provided by head of respective school in order to coordinate with organizer.To download coordinator details form Click here.
  • Resgistration fee: Rs 50/- per student(Non refundable).participating students must register through respective school, failing to which no participation will be entertained.
  • First round examination will be conducted in respective school if number of participating students will be greater than 50. If number of students will be less nearby school will be examination centre for first round.
  • Admit card is mandatory for all rounds examination. It will be issued to students after registraion.
  • Second round qualified students will be awarded with participation certificate. Winner and runner-up will be awarded cash prize money along with merit certificates
  • Top five group/individual student of round 2 will be qualified for final round.
  • Final round will be open staze competition
  • Decision of Quiz Master will be final.
  • Organizers holds rights to change rule and regulations of Quiz. Prior information will be provided to respective school event coordinator/students
  • School having highest number of participating students will be awarded by Organizing school.
