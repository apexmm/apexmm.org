A.P.S. Inter School Events Carnival 2017

A.P.S. Inter School Events Carnival 2017 is annual competition fest of Apex Public School, Marang Marcha. It is conducted on the ocassion of "Foundation Day". The grand finale of this events carnival is conducted on nearby sunday of 3rd October, so that more numbers of students can be the part of this mega event. Under this carnival numbers of events are conducted such as Quiz Competition, Essay Writting, Singing, Dance Competition (Single & Group), Speech Competition, Painting etc. All the events are conducted from school level to state level. In every events a student or group of students are selected from preliminary round (School level) for further rounds of particular event. The purpose of organizing these events is to provide a platform to compete with students of different school and show their eternal talents to the world. The aim of this carnival is to prepare students for different competitions which they will face in their students life, from the initial staze of their students life. Winner and runner-up of each events will be awarded with scholarship amount and merit certificates. All details related to events are mentioned below:

General rules and regulations:

  • School registration is mandatory. Without registration of school no students of respective school will be eligible for participating in any events.
  • Resgistration fee: Rs 50/- per student. A student can participate in maximum three events. Registration fee will be non refundable.
  • Admit card will be issued to students after registartion. Without admit card no students will be allowed to sit for Round 1.
  • It is necessary to provide event coordinator's name and details of respective school by the head of the institution/governing bodies for the communicating purpose of events.
  • First round of every events will be at respective school level, if number of participants from respective school will be more than 50. Otherwise first round examination may be conducted at nearby school having number of participants more than 50.
  • First round of every event of respective school will be conducted by respected school after the dates provided by organizer under the super vision of event coordinator of respective school.
  • School having maximum number of participation of students will be awarded with "Maximum participation award" by the organizing school.
  • Grand finale of all events will be held at Apex Public School, Marang Marcha.
  • Organizing school holds the right of changing rules and regulations of the events as per requirements.
  • Scholarship amount: Rs 20,000/- (including all events).

Quiz Competition:

  • Student can participate in group or single. A group of two student can be formed with the student belonging to same group of respective school.
  • Top two students or groups will be selected from each class category (V-VII & VIII-XII) for eliminatrion round on the basis of marks scored in Round 1 test.
  • No replacement will be done in case of any absentees on the day of first round examination. If any body is found doing so, registration of particular school will be cancelled. Further no events will be conducted on particular school.
  • Selected candidates of first round of all school will appear in the elimination round. Top 5 group/students will be selected for grand finale of quiz on the basis of marks scored in elimination round.
  • First round test will be written.
  • Grand finale will be oral. It will be conducted on open staze.
  • The decision of "Quiz Master" will be final.

Syllabus for Quiz Test:

  • Basic English (including grammar).
  • Reasoning.
  • Aptitude.
  • Basic mathematics.
  • Basic computer.

English Speech, Dance competition:

  • Top 2 students will be selected from first round for speech and dance respectively.
  • First round selected candidates will be appear directly for grand finale.
  • Winner and runner-up will be decided on the basis of performance on grand finale.
  • For grand finale speech topic will be declared earlier. Student need to prepare on respective topics.
  • Winner and runner-up of first round dance competition will participate in final round.

English Essay Writing Competition:

  • Topic will be provided.
  • Top 2 essay will be selected from first round.
  • Winner and runner-up will be choosen among all the selected students essay from all participating school.
