A.P.S. Inter School Events Carnival 2017

A.P.S. Inter School Events Carnival 2017 is annual competition fest of Apex Public School, Marang Marcha. It is conducted on the ocassion of "Foundation Day". The grand finale of this events carnival is conducted on nearby sunday of 3rd October, so that more numbers of students can be the part of this mega event. Under this carnival numbers of events are conducted such as Quiz Competition, Essay Writting, Singing, Dance Competition(Single & Group), Speech Competition, Painting etc. All the events are conducted from school level to state level. In every events a student or group of students are selected from preliminary round(School level) for further rounds of particular event. The purpose of organizing these events is to provide a platform to compete with students of different school and show their eternal talents to the world. The aim of this carnival is to prepare students for different competitions which they will face in their students life, from the initial staze of their students life. Winner and runner-up of each events will be awarded with scholarship amount and merit certificates. All details related to events are mentioned below:
