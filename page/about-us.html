About Us

Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School, Marang Marcha was established on 3rd October 2014. The school code is 20241501103 The school aims at giving a qualitatively high standard of education which would develop the student innate potential to the fullest. The school is a co-educational English medium School. The medium of instruction is English. The class rooms are large, airy and well lit tucked away from noise. The student activity center in contrast is compact, cheerful and engaging. The campus sports & cultural facilities are extensive. The best of education is given with utmost care love and understanding with a view to instilling into these young minds moral & ethical values, strength of character, discipline, national outlook and abiding spirit of service & sacrifice, thus helping and encouraging them to develop a broader & healthier outlook in life by providing the right kind of educational nourishment. Join us to make your child's future better.

Vision:

Apex prepares the students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, by imparting quality education among them by enhancing their learning aptitude and gearing up them with intellectual, physical, analytical and practical capabilities and thus make your child's future better.
