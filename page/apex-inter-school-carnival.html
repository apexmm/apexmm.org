APEX Interschool Carnival 2017

"APEX Interschool Carnival 2017" is annual competition fest organized by Apex Public School, Marang Marcha in cooperation with Birsa Social Academy, Gola started in the year 2015. In first sesion this event was conducted under the name tag "APEX Fiesta". This mega carnival is named as "APEX INTERSCHOOL CARNIVAL" in second edition. Under APEX Interschool Carnival 2017 Quiz Competition, Essay Writing ( English & Hindi), Speech, Dance Competition (Single & Group), Painting & Chess competition will be conducted at state level. In every events a student or group of students will be selected from preliminary round (School level) for further rounds of particular event. The purpose of organizing this carnival is to promote competitative nature in between students. This carnival provide a platform to compete with students, starting from respective school, finally students with different school. This carnival provides a open platform to students to show their eternal talents to the world. Grand finale of carnival is conducted on the ocassion of " APEX Foundation Day". It is conducted on near by sunday of 3rd October. Winner and runner-up of each events will be awarded with scholarship amount and merit certificates. All details related to events are mentioned below:

Eligibility:

Students of class V-class XII are eligible for participating in different events of carnival. Students can be from any board such as CBSE, ICSE, JAC.

Events Details

This year following events will be held in "APEX Interschool Carnival".

  • Quiz Competition
  • Essay writing Competition ( Hindi & English)
  • Dance Competition: Group and Single
  • Drawing/painting Competition
  • Speech Competition
  • Chess Competition

General rules and regulations:

  • School registration is mandatory. Without registration of school no students of respective school will be eligible for participating in any events.
  • Resgistration fee: Rs 50/- per event.Registration fee will be non refundable.
  • Admit card will be issued to students after registartion. Without admit card no students will be allowed to sit for any round of the events.
  • It is necessary to provide event coordinator's name and details of respective school by the head of the institution/governing bodies for the communicating purpose of events.
  • School having maximum number of participation of students will be awarded with "Maximum participation award" by the organizing school.
  • Grand finale of all events will be held at Apex Public School, Marang Marcha.
  • Organizing school holds the right of changing rules and regulations of the events as per requirements.
  • If any students found doing some activity which violates the rules and regulations of the carnival, He/She will be disqualified. No reuest will be entertained
  • Scholarship amount: Rs 20,000+ (including all events).

Mode of payment:

Registration fee of participating students will be collected by respective school coordinator. Coordinator need to submit registration fee at Apex Public School and complete registration process. After registration all process of conducting events at respective will start.

Quiz Competition:

  • This event will be conducted in two group:
    1. Group 1: Class V - Class VII
    2. Group 2: Class VIII - Class XII
  • Student can participate in group of two or single. A group of two student can be formed with the student belonging to same group of respective school.
  • Top two students or groups will be selected from each class category (V-VII & VIII-XII) for eliminatrion round on the basis of marks scored in Round 1 test.
  • No replacement will be done in case of any absentees on the day of first round examination. If any body is found doing so, registration of particular school will be cancelled. Further no events will be conducted on particular school.
  • First round qualified students of all school will appear in the elimination round. Top 5 group/students will be selected for grand finale of quiz on the basis of marks scored in elimination round.
  • First round test will be written and conducted at respective school or nearby examination centre. Examination centre will be specified in Admit card.
  • Grand finale will be oral. It will be conducted on open staze using projector.
  • The decision of "Quiz Master" will be final.

Syllabus for Quiz Test:

  • Basic English (including grammar).
  • Reasoning.
  • Aptitude.
  • Basic mathematics.
  • Basic computer.

English Speech Competition:

  • First Round: In first round two students will be selected from respective school. Selection will be done by respective school. Selected candidates will participate in final round.
  • Topic for final round speech will be declared after the publication of first round result.
  • The list of all participating students must be sent to organizer by respective school, failing to which first round result of respective school will not be published.

Essay Writing Competition:

  • In this students need to send their write up to organizer through respective school. The topics for first round are .........
  • Top 2 essay will be selected from first round. Selected students will participate in final round.
  • The list of all participating students must be sent to organizer by respective school, failing to which first round result of respective school will not be published.
  • Respective school can send write up by post or can submit directly at the oranizing school office.

Dance Competition:

Respective school need to send 2 single/Group dance performers. There will be directly final round for this event.

Drawing Competition:

  • First round: All participating students of respective school need to do drawing in White Chart paper(full size).
  • Theme for first round drawing is .............
  • Top two drawing will be selected among the drwaings of first round. Selected students result will be published in the site.
  • Final round: Final round of this event will be held at Apex Public School, Marang Marcha. Selected students will be assigned a theme for painting and need to do at same time.
  • Students need to bring all related equipments/stationaries for final round.
